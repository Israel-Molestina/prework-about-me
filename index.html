What's your dream job?

To be honest, I don’t have a "dream job". I believe that the 40 hour work week that this culture worships was the creation of capitalists pigs, and the notion of a "dream job" is a way to trick the common people into buying into the idea of working until you die for the profit of a few billionaires. In reality, humans are so much more than work every day at your "dream job" for a few vacation days a year and good health care (which the government should be providing anyways). I refuse to believe my purpose is to work myself to death 40+ hours a week until I’m 65 and have nothing to show for it other than material objects I've bought over the years. But I am not naive and realize that there is not much I can do by myself, and at least for the time being I have to play along. So, my dream job is one that pays me well, does not overwork me and treats me as a human being and not data on a spreadsheet.
